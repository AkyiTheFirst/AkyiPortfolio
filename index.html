About Myself

I am a self-taught digital artist, who does a little bit of graphic design and some traditional art. On top of doing digital art, I am also a computer science student with strong basic coding knowledge. I have already learned languages such as C, Java, Python and JavaScript. I have also worked as a coding coach at CIY.CLUB Myanmar where I teach block-coding and basic electronics to younger generation. During my time as a coach, I have also been trained to be proficient in 3D design using Tinkercad Website.

Some of My Digital Art

Mandalay Kyone : Mandalay is where I feel my true freedom. I illustrated this when I was away from the place I love most.

Fantasia Mushrooms : I've watched Disney's fantasia as a kid and was obsessed with this scene. These mushrooms danced to the "Chinese Dance" from "Tchaicovsky's Nutcracker Suite".

Pixel Stars : I love both pixelart and stars. So this pixelart became my favourite for a while. My favourite part about pixelart is learning how to use colors to achieve blended effect.

Seashore : Apart from stars, I love being in water. I love swimming especially in the sea. For me, water indicates freedom. Letting things flow as they pass by, not being attached to anything.

Excalibur : As I am a huge fun of classic Disney, "The Sword in the Stone" became another inspiration for me to illustrate this piece. I wanted it to be whimsical and dreamy and I am satisfied with the results.

The Moon : For someone who admires the moon, I gifted this pixelart as a loving gift. I feel peaceful when I look at this piece, just like how he makes me feel.
